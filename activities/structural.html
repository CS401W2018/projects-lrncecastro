Questions

What goes in the header?

- Website Name with a Logo

What goes in the Nav?

-Things that goes to NAV are the things that is important to the website such as about, contact, sign in/sign up, and other links that a user can easily navigate.

Can the Nav go in the header?

-Yes, but it's very rare because header is usually the first thing that a user can see so more often the NAV should go at the bottom of the header.

Does it have to go in the header?

- Yes, it has to go to the header for efficiency of the programmer and conveniency of the user.

Does the Nav contain links to another website?

- Yes

What goes in Main?

- The CONTEXT of the website or the CONTEXT of the link that is in nav.

What goes in the Footer?

- Some legal conditions, contact information, links related to the website.
