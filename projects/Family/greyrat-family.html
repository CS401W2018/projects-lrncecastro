  • The story started with Rudy. A shut in nerd who died in a car accident after buying something from a convenience store for saving a high school girl. He died and got reincarnated in a different world, where magic exist.

Anime: Mushuko Tensei

Mushuko Tensie

Paul Greyrat

He is Rudy's father, and husband of Zenith. Paul is a skilled swordsman, comes from the Greyrat Family, a respected family of adventurers. He is a complex character in the anime because of his character. However, he is also depicted as a loving and protective father to his children. He helped his son Rudy grow throughout the story.

Rudeus Greyrat

Rudeus Greyrat (Rudy) is a 34 year-old man in modern Japan. He shut himself in for a very long time because of depression came from bullying. However, after dying in an accident he got reincarnated as a baby of Paul and Zenith. Throughout the story Rudy strives to become a good person opposite of what he used to be before he died. His new life was also full of challenges but he never succumb to being depress instead he does his best to overcome them.

Zenith Greyrat

Zenith Greyrat is a supporting character in the anime Mushoku Tensei. She is Paul's wife and comes from a family of great magicians. She is good in using healing magic. Zenith is known for her strong, caring nature and is highly devoted to her family. Throughout the series, she is seen as a source of emotional support, offering guidance and love, especially when Rudeus needs it.
