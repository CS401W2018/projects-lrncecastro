Greyrat Family

  • Fictional family created in Japan
  • The picture is the one that all the viewers wanted to see.
  • The story started with Rudy. A shut in nerd who died in a car accident after buying something from a department store for saving a high school girl. He died and got reincarnated in a different world, where magic exist.

Paul Greyrat

He is Rudy's father, and husband of Zenith. Paul is a skilled swordsman, comes from the Greyrat Family, a respected family of adventurers. He is a complex character in the anime because of his character. He helped his son Rudy grow throughout the story.

Rudeus Greyrat

Rudeus Greyrat (Rudy) is a 34 year-old man in modern Japan. He shut himself in for a very long time because of depression came from bullying. However, after dying in an accident he got reincarnated as a baby of Paul and Zenith. Throughout the story Rudy strives to become a good person opposite of what he used to be before he died. His new life was also full of challenges but he never succumb to being depress instead he does his best to overcome them.

Zenith Greyrat

Zenith comes from a family of great magicians. She is good in using healing magic and once went to adventurer with Paul. Zenith is a gentle and nurturing person in short her personality is opposite to her husband. She is kind, loving and supportive mother to Rudy.
