Rimuru Tempest

Reincarnated as a Slime started with the protagonist named Satoru Mikami. He is a middle age man that worked as a General Contractor then due to an accident he got reincarnated in a different world where magic, demihumans, and devils exist. During the time he was laying down he thought a lot of things that resulted his form to be a slime.

Waking up in a different world as a slime he found himself inside of a cave where the Legendary Storm - Veldora was sealed. These 2 individuals became good friends which in the later episodes of the anime they fight enemies hand in hand.

As of the moment, Rimuru Tempest is the strongest anime character the world has ever created.

Shadow

In the anime The Eminence in Shadow Shadow is the main protagonist, his real name is Cid Kagenou. He is a young man obsessed in becoming a powerful and mysterious figure working behind the shadows, mvery uch like a fictional characters in his favorite stories.

Cid is very skilled in martial arts and strategy, but he deliberately hides his true abilities, choosing to act as if he is just a normal, average person. One night due to his obsession he went to a forest to train but in dissapointment he smack his head to a rock making his head bled a lot. Out of his mind he jump to a coming car, and died through from a car accident.

He got reincarnated on a different world where Magic exists, there Cid started his training again and studies to become powerful. Right now Cid (Shadow) holds the power to obliterate the whole world if he desires through his power that he named, Atomic.

Sung Jinwoo

Sung Jinwoo is the central figure of the anime "Solo Leveling," at the beginning of the anime Jinwoo is very infamous of being the weakest hunter in the world because he is placed in the lowest of E-Rank Hunters. However, his life took a dramatic turn following an encounter of a unique dangerous double dungeon, where he obtained "the System," a unique ability that enables him to level up his strength.

This new power he acquired propels him on an extraordinary journey, charting his evolution from the weakest of Hunters to an immensely powerful entity. Throughout his progression, Jinwoo demonstrates a strong protective instinct towards his family and a remarkable adaptability, quickly mastering the intricacies of his newfound abilities and facing increasingly dangerous challenges.
