Title and Topic of Site

Title: Overpower Anime Main Characters

Topic: Introducing some overpower anime main characters.

Reason: I opted to make a website about the best animes I watched this semester, because these animes help me relax during the times I feel overwhelm with homeworks and projects. Moreover, I chose this topic because watching animes is one of the closest thing to my heart. I started watching animes when I was still a little boy, and I can vividly recall that I even used to go to our neighbor so I could watch with them. And now having an access to the internet and having the means to watch and enjoy it, I will spoil myself more.
