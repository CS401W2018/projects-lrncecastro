Suggested Anime Movies

Demon Slayer: The Entertainment District Arc

The Demon Slayer: Entertainment District Arc is not a standalone movie but rather a part of the second season of the anime series. It follows Tanjiro Kamado and his friends Zenitsu Agatsuma and Inosuke Hashibira as they team up with Tengen Uzui, the Sound Hashira, to infiltrate the Yoshiwara Entertainment District. Their mission is to uncover and defeat the demon responsible for the disappearance of Tengen's wives, who were sent to investigate earlier. The demon they are hunting is Daki, the Upper Rank Six Demon Moon, who is hiding in the district's brothels. The arc is known for its intense battles and emotional depth, as it explores the characters' personal struggles and relationships, particularly Tengen's bond with his wives.

The Entertainment District Arc is marked by its visually stunning animation and thrilling action sequences. The story delves into themes of loyalty, sacrifice, and the complexities of human relationships. Tengen Uzui is a central figure in this arc, and his character development highlights his unique personality and backstory. The climax of the arc involves a dramatic battle against Daki and her brother Gyutaro, who emerges from her body after she is beheaded. The demon slayers face immense challenges as they struggle to defeat both demons simultaneously. Ultimately, they succeed in beheading the siblings, but not without significant consequences, including Tengen's decision to retire from the Demon Slayer Corps due to his injuries

Your Name

Your Name (Kimi no Na wa) is a 2016 Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai. The movie tells the story of two high school students, Taki Tachibana from Tokyo and Mitsuha Miyamizu from the rural town of Itomori, who mysteriously begin to swap bodies despite having never met. This phenomenon occurs intermittently, with each waking up in the other's body on certain days. They communicate through notes and messages, influencing each other's lives significantly. Mitsuha, in Taki's body, helps him become more confident at work and in his relationships, while Taki, in Mitsuha's body, assists her in becoming more popular at school.

As the story unfolds, Taki and Mitsuha develop strong feelings for each other, but their connection is complicated by a temporal twist. It is revealed that Mitsuha lives in 2013, while Taki lives in 2016. Taki discovers that Mitsuha's town was destroyed by a comet fragment three years before his time. Determined to save her, Taki uses the body-swapping phenomenon to communicate with Mitsuha's past self, helping her to evacuate the town before the disaster. Although they succeed in saving Itomori, their memories of each other fade. Years later, they coincidentally meet in Tokyo, feeling an inexplicable connection, and ask each other for their names, symbolizing a new beginning

Haikyu: The Dumpster Battle

Haikyu!! The Dumpster Battle is a 2024 Japanese animated sports film directed and written by Susumu Mitsunaka, based on Haruichi Furudate's manga series Haikyu!!. The film is a direct sequel to the anime television series and follows the long-awaited match between Karasuno High School and Nekoma High School, known as the "Dumpster Battle," in the third round of the Spring Nationals. This match is significant as it marks the first official competition between the two schools, with Shoyo Hinata and Kenma Kozume finally facing off against each other in a high-stakes game.

The film features an ensemble cast, including Ayumu Murase as Shoyo Hinata, Kaito Ishikawa as Tobio Kageyama, and Yuki Kaji as Kenma Kozume. It was released in Japan on February 16, 2024, by Toho and later in the United States on May 30, 2024, by Crunchyroll through Sony Pictures Releasing. Many watchers have expressed their thought that their emotions was like a roller coaster during the movie for both team wanted to win the game and at the same time enjoy it.
