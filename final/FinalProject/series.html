Suggested Anime Series

If you are new to watching anime, here are my recommendations to get you started on the right foot. Begin with something light but very interesting story with an overpower main character, That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime. If you are looking for a chill and funny anime Eminence of Shdow is your way to go. If you are a person who loves action but the main character started as a weak person Solo Leveling and Ragna Crimson. But what if you like playing videos and sometimes you yourself wanted to be the character that you created, Overlord is for you where the main character became the person he created from his video game. Of course Magic is something that everyone like The World's Finest Assassin is for you where the MC was originally an assassin before he died and get reincarnated here you can see how he will use magic in his assassinations.
